Setting the foreground color to go with the background colors wasn’t absolutely necessary, but it’s always a good idea. Now we have two non-floated elements (#wrapper, #content) with no visible background, and a page with a white background. You might be worried that Navigator 4.x does terrible things with the value transparent, but be of good cheer: NN4.x only pays attention to style sheets that have a media value of screen. Just like with @import, the styles in our print style sheet are forever hidden from Navigator 4’s rheumy eyes. So no worries there.
